Rocky Point's most-loved bar, open every day. Good vibes, cold drinks, and a staff that will know your name.

Find Us See the Menu

Rocky Point has a lot of bars. Bottoms Up is the one people come back to every weekend.

Outdoor patio with string lights and a fire pit stage. Pool tables. Cold drinks. And a staff that genuinely wants you to feel at home. We're open seven days a week and we love having you here.

Why People Keep Coming Back

The Drinks

Bloody Marys, mimosas, and coffee drinks are what people talk about most. Full bar, made right, priced fairly. Come in the morning or come in the evening.

The Patio

Blue sofas, string lights, a pergola overhead, and a fire pit stage where live bands play every Friday. This is the kind of outdoor space you look forward to coming back to.

The Vibe

Pool tables, Giant Jenga, foosball, Mexican Train Dominoes, karaoke Thursdays, Chickin Sh*t Bingo on Saturdays. Danny puts it well: it works for a work meeting just as well as a night out with friends.
