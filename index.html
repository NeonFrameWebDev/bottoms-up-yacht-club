Rocky Point has a lot of bars. Bottoms Up is the one people come back to every weekend.

Outdoor patio. Fire pit. Pool tables. Cold drinks. And a staff that will know your name by your second visit. We're open seven days a week, and we're always happy to see you.

Why People Keep Coming Back

The Drinks

Bloody Marys. Mimosas. Coffee drinks. Cocktails that are actually good. No bad pours here.

The Patio

String lights. Blue sofas. A fire pit with a stage. The kind of outdoor space you didn't expect to find in Rocky Point. Come for one drink. Stay all afternoon.

The Vibe

Pool tables. Giant Jenga. Foosball. Karaoke Thursdays. Chickin Sh*t Bingo on Saturdays. Conducive to work meetings, Mexican Train Dominoes, or meeting up with your buddies. Danny's words, not ours.
