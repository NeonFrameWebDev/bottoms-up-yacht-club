The Bar

  • Bloody Mary Our best seller, and for good reason. House-made and loaded. Exactly what you want on a Rocky Point morning.
  • Mimosas Classic and fresh. Goes with everything on this menu and most times of day.
  • Coffee Drinks Hot, iced, or spiked. We open at 10 AM and the coffee drinks are worth coming in for on their own.
  • Cocktails Full bar, made with care. Ask your bartender for a recommendation and they'll have one ready.
  • Beer and Seltzers Ice cold. Bottles, cans, and whatever's on draft.

Bar Bites and Food

Egg Roll Special Served daily and the single most-ordered food item on the menu. People drive back to Rocky Point just to have these again.
Hamburgers A real burger, made with care. One of the top sellers and a solid reason to come in for lunch.
Nachos Loaded the right way, with eggs and red salsa. The kind you actually share with the table.
Burritos Ask your server for today's fillings. They change and they're always good.
Club Sandwich A classic done well, with fries. Satisfying and exactly what you want after a morning on the water.
Pozole Homestyle, the real thing. A full bowl that earns every bit of the attention it gets.
