The Story

September 1, 2023.

The day Bottoms Up Yacht Club opened its doors in Rocky Point. Also the day Jimmy Buffett died.

Opening

The next logical thing.

Danny Davis didn't plan it that way, but there's something right about it. His grandfather had been coming to Puerto Penasco since the 1960s. Danny grew up with Rocky Point in his blood and a Margaritaville tattoo to prove it. Opening a bar here wasn't a business plan. It was the next logical thing.

What It Is

A neighborhood bar. Danny calls us a community center, and he's not wrong.

Come in once and you're a regular. Come in twice and the staff knows your name. People drive down from Arizona every weekend. They bring their friends. Those friends start coming on their own. That's how this works.

We've got a full indoor bar with a brick face and a bottle wall. A pool room for locals. A patio with blue sofas, string lights, and a fire pit stage where bands play on Fridays. Karaoke on Thursdays. Chickin Sh*t Bingo on Saturdays. Games everywhere: pool, Jenga, foosball, dominoes, chess.

We are open seven days a week. We are always happy to see you.

"We're a neighborhood bar -- a community center where everyone knows each other or feels at home." Danny Davis, managing partner
The Feel

"Our staff knows you by name and you feel at home -- not just like a one-time tourist."

That's Danny. That's the whole point.

Good attitudes and good vibes are the actual policy here. Not a slogan. Just how it is.

We've got a 4.9 on Google across 170 reviews. We're proud of that. We're more proud that people keep coming back.
